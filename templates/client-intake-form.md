# Client Intake Form

Send this as a Google Form or simple email questionnaire after the contract is signed. The point is to get everything you need to start building **before** you sit down to build.

---

## About the business

1. **Business name** (exactly as you want it shown on the site)
2. **What does the business do?** (one paragraph in your own words)
3. **Years in business**
4. **Address** (street, city, state, ZIP — exactly as it should appear)
5. **Phone number** — the one customers should call
6. **Email** — the one that should get contact-form submissions
7. **Hours of operation** (per day)
8. **Service area** (city, county, "within 30 miles," etc.)

## About your customers

9. **Who is your typical customer?** (age range, income level, what brings them in)
10. **What's the #1 thing you want them to do on the site?** (call · book · order online · stop in · request a quote)
11. **What's the #2 thing?**
12. **What questions do you get asked over and over?** (these become FAQ entries)

## About the brand

13. **Do you have a logo?** If yes, attach the highest-quality file you have (PNG, SVG, or a clear photo of a printed sign). If no, do you want one designed? (+$150 redraw fee for Starter plan, included in Plus and Pro)
14. **Brand colors** — paste hex codes if you know them, or attach a photo of your storefront/menu/business card
15. **Three websites you like** (any industry, just sites that *feel right* to you)
16. **One thing you DON'T want the site to feel like** ("not corporate," "not flashy," etc.)

## Content

17. **5–10 photos** — interior, exterior, products, food, team. Phone photos are fine. **Drop them in this folder:** {Google Drive / Dropbox link}
18. **Your "About" story** — 2–4 sentences on why the business exists. Don't write a novel. I'll polish.
19. **Testimonials or Google reviews you'd like featured** (paste 2–4 if you have them)
20. **Anything currently on your old website you want to keep?** (specific pages, blog posts, photos)

## Technical

21. **Domain name** — do you already own one? If so, where is it registered (GoDaddy, Cloudflare, Network Solutions, etc.)? If not, what name do you want? (I'll register it on Cloudflare in your name)
22. **Existing email setup** — are you using Google Workspace, Microsoft, or something else? (Don't worry about this if you don't know — we'll figure it out before launch.)
23. **Social media profiles** to link from the footer (Facebook, Instagram, Google)
24. **Online ordering / booking system** in use today? (Toast, Square, Vagaro, etc.) — only matters for Plus/Pro
25. **Any third-party services to integrate?** (Mailchimp, QuickBooks, etc.)

## Launch

26. **Target launch date** — when do you need this live? (Be honest, not optimistic.)
27. **Anything seasonally important?** ("must launch before Memorial Day," "before tax season," etc.)
